## Overview
2C-B was synthesized by Alexander "Sasha" Shulgin in 1974 and described in his seminal book *PiHKAL: A Chemical Love Story* (1991). It was legally sold as "Nexus" in sex shops and head shops until it was scheduled in 1994.

2C-B is widely considered to have the best balance of psychedelic effects and manageable headspace of any classical psychedelic. Visuals are often described as the most beautiful and colorful of any substance — highly aesthetic, sensual, erotic.

The dose-response curve is steep: small dose increases produce dramatically different experiences.

## Effects
**Visual:** The defining feature. Intense, colorful, highly aesthetic geometric patterns and visuals. Colors become incredibly vivid. Surfaces "breathe" and shimmer. Considered by many to have the most beautiful visuals of any psychedelic.

**Headspace:** Notably clearer than LSD or psilocybin at comparable doses. Less overwhelming, more controllable. Sensory enhancement without heavy thought loops.

**Empathogenic:** At moderate doses, strong empathogen effect comparable to MDMA. Popular as a "last night" addition to multi-day festivals after MDMA nights.

**Aphrodisiac:** Strong pro-sexual effects. Touch, music, and sexual experience are dramatically enhanced.

**Physical:** Some nausea on comeup, stimulation, pupil dilation, mild cardiovascular stimulation

## Dosage
| Level | Dose (oral) | Effects |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Threshold | 5–10mg | Subtle |
| Light | 10–15mg | Mild visuals, mood lift |
| Common | 15–25mg | Full experience — visuals, empathy, altered cognition |
| Strong | 25–35mg | Intense visuals, ego softening |
| Heavy | 35mg+ | Full ego dissolution, intense, anxiety possible |

**Insufflated:** ~1.5–2x more potent. 10mg insufflated ≈ 15–20mg oral. Also known to be EXTREMELY painful — described as one of the most painful insufflation experiences of any drug. Not recommended.

**Steep dose-response:** The difference between 15mg and 25mg is significant. Start low, especially with new batches.

## Duration Breakdown
- **Onset (oral):** 45–75 min
- **Come-up:** 30–45 min (can be uncomfortable)
- **Peak:** 2–3 hr
- **Plateau:** 1–2 hr
- **Come-down:** 1–2 hr
- **Total:** 4–6 hr
- **Afterglow:** Few hours (pleasant)

Much shorter than LSD or mescaline — a full 6hr experience with clean after-effects.

## Pharmacology
2C-B is a partial agonist at 5-HT2A, 5-HT2B, and 5-HT2C receptors. Also has activity at several other serotonin receptor subtypes. The combined agonism at multiple receptor subtypes is thought to contribute to its distinctive character.

Does not build tolerance rapidly. Cross-tolerant with other serotonergic psychedelics.

## Drug Combinations
| Combination | Safety | Notes |
|-------------|--------|-------|
| MDMA ("candy flip" / "blossom") | Caution | Very popular. Timing matters — typically 2C-B taken 3–4hr after MDMA peak. Extremely beautiful but physically demanding. |
| Cannabis | Caution | Significantly amplifies visuals and can cause anxiety. |
| Alcohol | Caution | Nausea, reduced experience, dehydration. |
| Lithium | Dangerous | Avoid with all psychedelics. |
| MAOIs | Caution | Prolongs and intensifies. Serotonin syndrome risk. |
| LSD | Caution | Very powerful combination. |
| Benzodiazepines | Rescue use | Reduces or stops experience if needed. |

## Harm Reduction
- **Steep dose curve:** First-time users should start at 10–15mg. The difference between doses is dramatic.
- **Set and setting:** Same as all psychedelics
- **Comeup discomfort:** The 45–75 min onset with its uncomfortable comeup (nausea, anxiety) is a known issue. Having ginger available helps. Commitment once consumed — trying to "abort" mentally worsens comeup anxiety.
- **Purity testing:** Mecke reagent (turns blue/green), Marquis reagent. Many "2C-B" sold as "pink cocaine" in South America is actually just pink ketamine or cutting agents — unreliable market.
- **Contraindications:** Personal/family history of psychosis, schizophrenia, bipolar I. Lithium. MAOIs.
