# DXM (Dextromethorphan)

tags: dissociative, otc, dxm, robitussin, cough syrup, robotripping, legal
category: dissociative

## Quick Facts
- **Type:** Dissociative / Sigma agonist
- **Legal status:** Legal OTC in USA (cough suppressant in DXM-only products)
- **Duration:** 4-6 hours (varies by dose; plateau 4 is much longer)
- **Onset:** 30-60 minutes
- **Common sources:** Robitussin DM (DXM only), Delsym, Coricidin (AVOID — see warning)

## Overview

Dextromethorphan (DXM) is a cough suppressant sold OTC in hundreds of products.
At therapeutic doses (15-30mg), it suppresses the cough reflex. At recreational
doses it becomes a powerful dissociative drug with effects ranging from mild
euphoria to complete ego dissolution and out-of-body experiences.

DXM works primarily as an NMDA receptor antagonist — the same mechanism as
ketamine and PCP, though with additional activity at sigma-1 receptors and
serotonin reuptake inhibition that make its profile unique.

The term "plateaus" is used to describe dose-dependent levels of effect.

## The Four Plateaus

### First Plateau (100-200mg)
- Mild euphoria and mood lift
- Slight perceptual changes; color enhancement
- Increased empathy (similar to low-dose MDMA)
- Mild dissociation; music enhancement
- Coordination slightly impaired

### Second Plateau (200-400mg)
- Strong euphoria and dissociation
- Visual effects: patterns, tracers, geometric visuals (eyes closed)
- Significant cognitive alteration
- "Robowalk" — distinctive altered gait
- Body feels disconnected from mind
- Strong music appreciation

### Third Plateau (400-700mg)
- Intense dissociation approaching K-hole
- Strong hallucinatory visuals (OEV and CEV)
- Ego dissolution begins
- Significant loss of motor control
- Out-of-body sensations
- Time distortion extreme
- Not safe to move around; stay in one place

### Fourth Plateau (700mg+)
- Complete ego dissolution and loss of identity
- No connection to physical body
- Possible "sigma realm" experiences — encounters with entities, other dimensions
- Very long duration (8-12+ hours)
- Serious risks; not recommended for most users
- Difficult or impossible to communicate or seek help if needed

## Dosage

**Critical note:** Body weight affects dosing significantly.

| Plateau | Dose (mg) | mg/kg |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | 100-200mg | 1.5-2.5 mg/kg |
| 2nd | 200-400mg | 2.5-7.5 mg/kg |
| 3rd | 400-700mg | 7.5-15 mg/kg |
| 4th | 700mg+ | 15+ mg/kg |

## Pharmacology

- **NMDA glutamate antagonist** (primary mechanism) — same as ketamine, PCP
- **Sigma-1 receptor agonist** — unique to DXM; contributes to hallucinatory effects
- **Serotonin reuptake inhibitor** — mild SSRI-like activity; empathogenic effects
- **CYP2D6 metabolized** — some people are poor metabolizers; DXM is much stronger
  and longer-lasting for them

## Drug Combinations — CRITICAL

| Combination | Risk | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| MAOIs | **LETHAL** | Serotonin syndrome; can be rapidly fatal. Includes Syrian rue, harmaline |
| SSRIs / SNRIs | **VERY HIGH** | Serotonin syndrome risk; very common combination mistake |
| Lithium | **HIGH** | Can cause seizures; avoid |
| Tramadol | **HIGH** | Serotonin syndrome risk |
| Alcohol | **HIGH** | Severe CNS depression; vomiting + dissociation = aspiration risk |
| Opioids | HIGH | CNS depression, respiratory issues |
| Stimulants | Moderate | Cardiovascular strain; heart rate elevation |
| Cannabis | Low-moderate | Intensifies and can disorient; common combination |

## The Coricidin Warning (CRITICAL)

**NEVER use Coricidin HBP Cough & Cold for recreational DXM.**

Coricidin contains chlorpheniramine maleate (an antihistamine/anticholinergic)
in addition to DXM. At DXM recreational doses, the chlorpheniramine dose
becomes severely toxic, causing anticholinergic syndrome — confusion,
seizures, cardiac arrhythmias, hyperthermia, and death.

**Use only DXM-only products (check ingredients).**
Products that are safe: Robitussin DM, Delsym (extended release), generic
"DXM only" syrup or gels.

## Harm Reduction

- **Check every ingredient** — only use products with DXM as the sole active
  ingredient. Guaifenesin (expectorant) is generally safe but causes nausea.
- Test for CYP2D6 metabolism — if you feel very strong effects at low doses,
  you may be a poor metabolizer; reduce dose significantly
- Never combine with SSRIs, SNRIs, or MAOIs — serotonin syndrome is real and deadly
- Have a sober sitter for 3rd plateau and above
- Stay still and in a safe place for higher plateaus
- DXM causes significant nausea — ginger tea or Dramamine (not Benadryl) can help
- Extended-release forms (Delsym) last much longer — plan accordingly

## Neurotoxicity Concerns

DXM at very high doses may cause **Olney's Lesions** (vacuoles in brain tissue)
in animals. Human risk is unclear but widely discussed in the community.
The risk appears dose-dependent and correlates with excitotoxicity through
sigma receptor activation.

Recommendation: avoid very high doses (4th plateau) frequently. Regular 3rd
plateau+ use is not without risk.

## Addiction Potential

Moderate psychological addiction potential; lower physical dependence than
opioids or alcohol. Some users develop strong habits, particularly at lower
plateaus. Daily use is inadvisable.
