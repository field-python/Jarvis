## Overview
MDMA was first synthesized by Merck chemists in 1912, and rediscovered and popularized by Alexander "Sasha" Shulgin in the 1970s. Shulgin introduced it to psychotherapists, who used it extensively in couples counseling and individual therapy through the early 1980s.

It was scheduled in 1985 despite significant protest from therapists documenting its clinical benefits. It became a defining drug of rave culture in the late 1980s–90s.

MAPS (Multidisciplinary Association for Psychedelic Studies) has conducted Phase 3 clinical trials for PTSD treatment. The FDA granted Breakthrough Therapy designation. As of 2024, the FDA rejected approval (citing trial methodology) but research continues.

MDMA is primarily an empathogen/entactogen — it produces feelings of emotional openness, empathy, love, and connectedness rather than traditional psychedelic visuals.

## Effects
**Emotional:** Intense feelings of love, empathy, emotional openness, closeness with others, decreased fear and defensiveness
**Perceptual:** Mild sensory enhancement (music sounds incredible), subtle visuals at high doses, pupil dilation
**Physical:** Increased heart rate and blood pressure, jaw clenching/bruxism, hyperthermia, sweating, increased energy, decreased appetite, possible nausea on comeup
**Cognitive:** Reduced anxiety and rumination, emotional fluency, increased talkativeness
**After-effects:** "Tuesday blues" / comedown — serotonin depletion can cause depression, fatigue, irritability for 1–3 days after

## Dosage
| Level | Dose | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| Threshold | 40–60mg | Subtle effects |
| Light | 60–90mg | Gentle come-up, mood lift |
| Common | 75–125mg | Full empathogenic effects |
| Strong | 125–175mg | Intensified effects, more physical stress |
| Heavy | 175mg+ | Higher neurotoxicity risk, severe comedown |

**Redosing:** If redosing, take at most 50–75% of original dose, and only within the first 90 minutes of the initial dose. Redosing after 90 min significantly worsens comedown with minimal benefit.

**Body weight:** Roughly 1–1.5mg/kg is a reasonable starting point for an unknown batch.

## Duration Breakdown
- **Onset:** 30–60 min (60–90 min with full stomach)
- **Come-up:** 30–60 min
- **Peak:** 1.5–3 hr
- **Plateau/come-down:** 1–2 hr
- **Residual:** 12–24 hr (often pleasant afterglow, sometimes depression)

## Pharmacology
MDMA causes massive release of serotonin (primary), dopamine, and norepinephrine from presynaptic neurons. Also inhibits their reuptake (like an SSRI + releaser combined). The serotonin surge is responsible for the empathogenic effects and the subsequent depletion responsible for comedowns.

**Neurotoxicity:** Frequent, high-dose use causes oxidative damage to serotonergic axons. Animal studies show significant damage at human-equivalent doses used repeatedly. The "three month rule" (use no more than once every 1–3 months) is based on research suggesting this as a minimum recovery window.

**Hyperthermia:** Overheating combined with physical exertion is a primary cause of MDMA-related hospitalization and death. Temperature regulation is impaired.

**Hyponatremia:** Drinking too much water (from fear of dehydration) has caused deaths — water poisoning. 500ml/hr when dancing; 250ml/hr when resting. No more.

## Harm Reduction
**Temperature:** Take breaks from dancing. Get out of the heat. MDMA impairs body temperature regulation. Overheating is a primary cause of death.

**Hydration:** Drink 500ml water per hour if dancing actively. Do NOT drink more — hyponatremia (water poisoning) is a real cause of death.

**Frequency:** Minimum 1–3 months between uses. Cumulative neurotoxicity with frequent use.

**Purity testing:** Use Marquis (purple/black = MDMA/MDA), Mecke, and Simon's reagents. Simon's differentiates MDMA (blue) from MDA (no reaction). Fentanyl test strips also recommended.

**Supplements (harm reduction, not scientifically proven):**
- Pre-loading: Vitamin C (antioxidant), Alpha Lipoic Acid (ALA), EGCG, magnesium glycinate (reduces jaw clenching)
- During: Vitamin C every 2hr
- Post-loading: ALA, EGCG, Vitamin C for 3 days after
- Avoid ALA during the roll itself (potentially increases neurotoxicity at that stage)

**Jaw clenching:** Magnesium 200-400mg before reduces bruxism significantly.

**Comedown support:** Good sleep, nutritious food, 5-HTP (serotonin precursor — start 12hr after last dose to avoid serotonin syndrome), B6, tryptophan-rich foods.

## Adulterants and Testing
The "molly"/"ecstasy" market has significant adulteration:
- **PMA/PMMA** (paramethoxymethamphetamine): Deadlier than MDMA, similar effects with longer onset — users redose thinking it "hasn't kicked in" and overdose. Tests orange/brown with Marquis.
- **Methamphetamine:** Tests orange-brown with Marquis.
- **Cathinones (bath salts):** Marquis turns orange-yellow. Simon's doesn't react.
- **NBOMes or other RCs:** Mecke, Marquis can help differentiate.

Always test before using. Fentanyl test strips are now recommended even for MDMA due to contamination cases.
