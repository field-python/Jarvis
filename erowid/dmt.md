## Overview
DMT is a tryptamine psychedelic that occurs endogenously in humans (in trace amounts in the pineal gland, lungs, and elsewhere) as well as in hundreds of plant species worldwide. It is the psychoactive component of ayahuasca, the South American shamanic brew.

Smoked DMT produces one of the most intense brief psychedelic experiences possible — complete dissociation from physical reality in under a minute, lasting 10–20 minutes total. Described as "more real than real," with geometric realms, entities, and profound ineffable experiences.

Oral DMT alone is inactive — destroyed by MAO enzymes in the gut. Combined with an MAOI (as in ayahuasca), it becomes orally active with a 4-6hr duration.

## Forms
**Freebase DMT (smoked/vaped):** White to yellow crystalline solid. Most common extracted form. Boiling point ~160°C. Vaporizes without combustion at correct temp.

**Changa:** DMT infused into plant material along with MAOIs (usually harmaline-containing plants like Banisteriopsis caapi or Syrian rue). Burns slower, effects slightly prolonged by MAOI content.

**Ayahuasca:** Traditional oral brew — DMT-containing plant (Psychotria viridis "chacruna" or Mimosa hostilis) + MAOI vine (Banisteriopsis caapi). Duration 4–6hr. Often includes purging (vomiting).

**Pharmahuasca:** Synthetic oral combination — encapsulated freebase DMT + MAOI (Syrian rue seeds or pharmaceutical harmine). Cleaner, more controlled than traditional brew.

## Effects (Smoked)
**0–30 sec:** Rushing onset, reality begins to distort, high-pitched tones
**30–60 sec:** Complete breakthrough — physical body and environment gone; immersion in geometric visual field
**1–5 min:** Peak — geometric architecture, entity contact, profound emotional/spiritual content, ego dissolution
**5–15 min:** Gradual return, reality slowly reassembles
**15–20 min:** Back to baseline; often profound afterglow

**Entities:** A consistent feature reported across cultures — encounters with beings described as aliens, elves, machine spirits, ancestors, gods. Mechanism unknown; experience is consistent across subjects.

**The "breakthrough":** At sufficient doses, complete loss of awareness of physical reality. Users report entering other dimensions, receiving communications, life reviews, death/rebirth experiences.

## Pharmacology
DMT is a full agonist at 5-HT2A and 5-HT2C receptors (same mechanism as psilocin). Also acts on sigma-1 receptors (unusual among psychedelics — may account for unique effects). Rapidly metabolized by MAO enzymes — hence the short duration when smoked and oral inactivity without MAOI.

Endogenous DMT is thought by some researchers to play a role in dreaming and near-death experiences (the "Strassman hypothesis"). Evidence is preliminary.

**Tolerance:** Minimal — DMT shows rapid physical tolerance but it resets within hours. Unlike LSD/psilocybin, users can repeat DMT experiences on the same day (though psychological saturation occurs).

| Combination | Safety | Notes |

## MAOI Protocol (For Oral DMT / Ayahuasca)
When using DMT orally with an MAOI — these rules are serious:

**Drugs to avoid (before and after — 2 weeks):**
- SSRIs, SNRIs, MAOIs (pharmaceutical)
- Tramadol, meperidine, dextromethorphan (DXM)
- MDMA, cocaine, amphetamines
- St. John's Wort
- Lithium

**Foods to avoid (tyramine-rich — hypertensive crisis risk with MAOIs):**
- Aged cheeses, cured/fermented meats (salami, pepperoni, aged sausage)
- Fermented foods (kimchi, sauerkraut, soy sauce in large amounts)
- Overripe or dried fruit
- Tap beer, some wines
- Fava beans

## Harm Reduction
- **Always lie down** before effects peak — complete physical incapacitation is common and sudden
- **Remove hazards** from the area (no furniture with sharp edges accessible if you flail)
- **Trip sitter strongly recommended** for breakthroughs
- **Never use with SSRIs and an MAOI** — serotonin syndrome risk
- **Cardiac caution:** DMT causes brief but significant blood pressure and heart rate increases
- **Psychological preparation:** Even 15 minutes of ego death can be profoundly disorienting — set intention beforehand
- **Integration:** Journaling and reflection after the experience helps process content

## Administration Methods
**Pipe/machine:** Glass oil burner heated from below. DMT placed in hot pipe and vapor inhaled. Most efficient but technique-dependent.

**Vaporizer:** Dedicated DMT vaporizers (Molecule, GVG-style) provide consistent temperature. Best efficiency.

**E-mesh pipe:** Electric resistance coil heats DMT on a mesh screen. Very efficient, consistent.

**Changa:** Packed like cannabis. Burns slower. More forgiving. MAOI in changa extends and modifies the experience.

**Do NOT combust DMT directly** — combustion destroys much of it and produces a harsh, less effective experience.

## Storage
Freebase DMT stores well. Sealed glass container + silica gel, cool dark place. Yellowing indicates mild oxidation (still active). 1+ years stable if properly stored.
