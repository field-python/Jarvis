# Winter Storm Profile

## Immediate Priorities

1. Confirm heat source and fuel status.
2. Charge batteries and power banks.
3. Fill water containers.
4. Check medications and first aid supplies.
5. Confirm communication plan and emergency contacts.

## Quick Checks

- indoor temperature
- flashlights and headlamps
- food for 72 hours minimum
- vehicle fuel and cold-weather kit
- backup cooking method
