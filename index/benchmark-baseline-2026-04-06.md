# PeePee Benchmark Baseline

Date: 2026-04-06

## Current Strengths

- good Alaska-specific flavor when Alaska notes are retrieved
- better citation handling than earlier versions
- "Next questions" is a strong feature
- strong coverage on water, winter, power outage, and food preservation basics
- improving coverage in governance, cyber security, ESP32, and Alaska hunting

## Current Weaknesses

- still vulnerable to weak retrieval on broad topics
- sometimes overuses summaries instead of richer primary pages
- occasional invented framing in improve mode
- some topic areas still have thin coverage compared to water/winter

## Immediate Benchmark Goal

Raise typical answers from mixed (`3`) to good (`4`) on:

- Alaska survival
- rebuilding society
- water / shelter / first aid
- hunting and wildlife
- cyber basics
- ESP32 basics
