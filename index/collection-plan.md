# Survival Archive Collection Plan

Use this as the priority list for what to save offline.

## Medical

- CPR and basic first aid guide
- wound cleaning and bandaging guide
- burns, fractures, sprains, and bleeding control references
- medication list with dosages and refill info
- allergies, blood types, emergency contacts, and provider details

## Water

- water purification methods
- bleach treatment ratios
- boil times and storage guidance
- local water sources and backup locations
- filter manuals for the gear you own

## Food

- shelf-stable food planning
- calorie planning tables
- cooking without grid power
- canning and dehydration references
- food rotation checklist

## Power

- battery charging and storage notes
- solar setup basics
- generator startup and maintenance
- fuel storage safety
- USB power bank and inverter notes

## Communications

- local emergency frequencies
- ham radio quick references
- repeater list
- family communication plan
- check-in schedule and message format

## Navigation

- local road maps
- topographic maps
- compass basics
- route cards for places you may need to reach
- coordinates for family, hospitals, fuel, water, and shelter points

## Repair

- tool inventory
- tarp, cordage, and shelter repair guides
- small engine troubleshooting
- tire change and jump start procedures
- basic wiring and fuse references

## Personal

- copies of IDs and insurance
- medication and vaccination records
- emergency contact sheet
- account recovery instructions

Sensitive material should be encrypted if stored here.
