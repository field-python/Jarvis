# Lemon Tek & Mushroom Preparation Methods

*Lemon tek is a preparation method that converts psilocybin to psilocin before ingestion using citric acid, resulting in faster onset, shorter but often more intense experiences, and reduced nausea.*

---

## What Is Lemon Tek?

**The chemistry:** Psilocybin (4-phosphoryloxy-DMT) must be converted to psilocin (4-hydroxy-DMT) in the body by alkaline phosphatase enzymes before it becomes active. The acidic pH of lemon juice (pH ~2) mimics stomach acid and partially performs this dephosphorylation externally, before you even drink it. The result is faster conversion in the gut and more uniform absorption.

**Effects compared to eating dried mushrooms:**
- **Onset:** 10-20 min (vs 30-60 min for dried)
- **Peak:** Hits harder and faster
- **Duration:** Often 3-4hr total (vs 4-6hr for dried)
- **Nausea:** Significantly reduced (chitin from mushroom cell walls is partially broken down by acid)
- **Intensity:** Many users report it feels 1.5-2x stronger at the same dose — likely due to faster absorption rate and higher peak psilocin blood levels
- **Comeup:** More sudden — be sitting down when it hits

**Dose adjustment:** If you normally take 2.5g dried, consider starting at 1.5-2g for lemon tek until you know how it affects you. The experience is compressed and more intense.

## Lemon Tek Method

### Ingredients
- Dried psilocybin mushrooms (finely ground)
- Fresh lemon juice (2 lemons, or equivalent bottled — must be citric acid, not "lemon flavoring")
- Optional: lime juice works equally well
- Shot glass or small cup

### Method

**Step 1 — Grind the mushrooms:**
- Grind dried mushrooms to a fine powder using a coffee grinder or pill crusher
- Finer grind = more surface area = better acid contact = faster conversion
- Remove any coarse bits that won't grind (stems have more chitin)

**Step 2 — Add citrus:**
- Place powder in shot glass
- Squeeze enough fresh lemon juice to fully submerge the powder
- Usually 1-2 tablespoons (enough to cover, not flood)
- Stir to combine — should look like a paste or slurry

**Step 3 — Wait:**
- Let sit for 15-20 minutes, stirring every 2-3 minutes
- The acid is converting psilocybin to psilocin during this time
- Liquid may turn slightly bluish/greenish — normal (psilocin oxidation)
- Some users extend to 30 minutes but 15-20 is sufficient

**Step 4 — Consume:**
- Option A: Drink the whole slurry (mushroom powder + liquid)
- Option B: Strain through cheesecloth or coffee filter — drink only the liquid (less nausea but slightly reduced potency — some psilocin sticks to the mushroom matter)
- Chase with water or juice to clear the taste
- Have your setting prepared. Onset is fast.

### Pro Tips
- Do this on an empty stomach (3-4hr fast) for best absorption
- Sit down after drinking — you may feel it within 10-15 minutes
- Some users add to tea after lemon soak for better palatability — ginger tea especially helps nausea
- Cold lemon juice works fine; heat is not needed and may degrade compounds
- Do not let it sit more than 45-60 minutes — psilocin begins to oxidize and degrade

## Mushroom Tea

*Gentler onset than raw mushrooms, much less nausea, easy to sip and pace the experience.*

**Method:**
1. Boil water, let cool to ~70°C (160°F) — do not use boiling water, heat degrades psilocybin above ~80°C
2. Grind dried mushrooms coarsely (not powder)
3. Steep in warm water for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally
4. Strain through cheesecloth/strainer — discard mushroom matter or eat it
5. Add ginger, honey, lemon to taste
6. Drink over 20-30 minutes

**Lemon + tea combo:** Some users do lemon tek then heat gently with water to make a warm drink — gets benefits of both. Keep temp low.

## Blue Honey

*Long-term storage + palatable form. Psilocin leaches into honey over time.*

**Method:**
1. Grind dried mushrooms very finely
2. Mix into raw honey at ratio of 1g mushrooms per tablespoon honey
3. Store in sealed jar in dark cool place for 2-4 weeks, stirring occasionally
4. Honey will turn blue — psilocin oxidation, normal
5. Dose by tablespoon according to your mushroom ratio

**Stability:** Honey is antimicrobial. Properly made blue honey stores for 1-2 years. The acids in honey may also perform partial lemon-tek-like conversion over time.

---

## Chocolates

**Method:**
1. Melt dark chocolate (70%+ cacao) in double boiler at 115-120°F max — psilocybin degrades above ~130°F
2. Remove from heat, let cool slightly to ~100°F
3. Mix in finely ground dried mushrooms thoroughly
4. Pour into molds (ice cube tray, candy molds)
5. Refrigerate until set
6. Dose by piece weight — weigh mold volume, calculate g mushrooms per piece

**Storage:** Refrigerator, wrapped — 6 months. Freezer — 1 year. Keep away from moisture.

## Nausea Reduction

Nausea is caused primarily by chitin in mushroom cell walls and by psilocybin conversion happening too rapidly. Reduction strategies:

1. **Lemon tek** — citric acid breaks down chitin, converts psilocybin before ingestion
2. **Mushroom tea (strained)** — leaves chitin behind in mushroom matter
3. **Ginger** — fresh ginger tea, ginger chews, or ginger capsules 30 min before
4. **Ginger + lemon tek combo** — sip lemon tek + ginger tea slowly
5. **Fasting** — empty stomach (4hr fast) actually reduces nausea for most people
6. **Lying still** — movement worsens nausea during onset; sit or lie quietly
7. **Activated charcoal** — not recommended (absorbs psilocybin too, reducing effects)

## Set and Setting Reminders

- **Intention:** Know why you're doing this. Write it down beforehand.
- **Environment:** Clean, comfortable, safe space. Remove hazards. Don't drive.
- **People:** Solo or with trusted people. One trip sitter for doses 2.5g+ equivalent.
- **Time:** Block out the full day + next morning. Don't schedule anything important.
- **Supplies:** Water, fruit, blanket, music playlist, bucket if nausea-prone, benzo for emergency (stops or blunts trip within 30-60 min)
- **Integration:** Journaling after a session extracts more insight from the experience.
