# DMT Extraction from Acacia Bark — MHRB/ACRB Methods

*N,N-Dimethyltryptamine (DMT) extraction from plant material. This documents the chemistry for educational purposes. The primary alkaloid target is N,N-DMT freebase.*

---

## Source Plants

**Acacia confusa root bark (ACRB)** — Taiwan/Vietnam source. One of the most commonly used. DMT content: 1-2% dry weight, primarily root bark. Inner root bark is more potent than outer.

**Other high-DMT Acacia species:**
- *Acacia obtusifolia* (Australia) — 0.3-1.8% root bark
- *Acacia acuminata* (Australia) — 1.0-1.8% root bark
- *Acacia maidenii* (Australia) — 0.36% stem bark
- *Mimosa hostilis* root bark (MHRB, now called *Mimosa tenuiflora*) — 0.57-1.7% — from Brazil/Mexico. Very popular source. Powdered form widely available.

**Note on β-carbolines:** Some Acacia species also contain harmine, harmaline, and tetrahydroharmine (β-carbolines, MAOIs). ACRB contains traces. MHRB is generally DMT-only. β-carboline content matters if making pharmahuasca/ayahuasca.

---

## Chemistry Overview

**DMT is a tryptamine alkaloid.** In plant material it exists as a salt (ionic, water-soluble). To extract:
1. **Basify** — raise pH to 11-13 with a base (NaOH or Ca(OH)₂). This converts DMT salt → DMT freebase (non-ionic, non-polar)
2. **Nonpolar solvent pull** — add a nonpolar solvent (naphtha, heptane, DCM). Freebase DMT preferentially migrates into the nonpolar layer.
3. **Separate layers** — decant or separate the nonpolar (top) layer
4. **Freeze precipitation OR evaporation** — crash DMT out of solvent as crystals

---

## Solvents

**Naphtha (VM&P Naphtha, lighter fluid):**
- Most commonly used. Available at hardware stores (Klean-Strip VM&P).
- Lower toxicity than DCM. Good DMT solubility.
- Boiling point ~150°C — evaporates safely outdoors.
- Poor for pulling N-oxide DMT (minor issue).

**Heptane:**
- Cleaner than naphtha. Pharmaceutical grade available online.
- Similar properties to naphtha. Good freeze precipitation results.

**Dichloromethane (DCM / methylene chloride):**
- Most efficient extraction. Pulls more alkaloids including N-oxide forms.
- Higher toxicity — avoid prolonged inhalation. More regulated.
- Denser than water — sinks to bottom layer (opposite of naphtha).

**Avoid:** Acetone (too polar), ethyl acetate (too polar), isopropyl alcohol (miscible with water).

---

## Bases

**Sodium Hydroxide (NaOH / lye):**
- Strong base. Effective at pH 13+.
- Available as drain cleaner (Roebic Crystal Drain Cleaner, Red Devil Lye).
- Generates significant heat when dissolved — add slowly, with stirring.
- Burns skin — gloves and goggles required.
- Creates sodium salts in solution.

**Calcium Hydroxide (Ca(OH)₂ / pickling lime / hydrated lime):**
- Weaker base — max pH ~12.4 in water.
- Safer than NaOH. Less caustic.
- Available at grocery stores (pickling lime) or hardware stores.
- Creates slightly muddy solution — slightly harder to separate cleanly.
- Preferred by many for safety profile.

## Full Extraction — STB (Straight to Base) Method

*Simplest method. Suitable for ACRB or MHRB powder. No acid prep step.*

### Equipment
- Large glass jar or HDPE bucket with lid (1-2 gallon)
- Separatory funnel or large graduated cylinder + turkey baster
- pH strips or pH meter
- Stainless steel or glass stirring rod
- Scale accurate to 0.1g
- Personal protective equipment: nitrile gloves, safety glasses, dust mask
- Ventilated workspace (outdoors or powerful fan venting outside)

### Materials
- 100g Acacia confusa root bark (ACRB) powder — starting amount for this guide
- 500ml distilled water (or more as needed)
- 50-60g NaOH (sodium hydroxide) OR 80-100g Ca(OH)₂
- 200ml naphtha (VM&P) — split into pulls

### Procedure

**Step 1 — Prepare the bark solution:**
1. Weigh 100g powdered ACRB into your large glass jar
2. Add 500ml distilled water
3. Stir thoroughly — bark should be fully submerged and wetted
4. Optional: let soak 30-60 min to ensure full hydration

**Step 2 — Basify:**
1. Put on gloves and eye protection
2. Pre-dissolve NaOH: add 50-60g NaOH slowly to 100ml water in a separate container, stir. It will heat up significantly — this is normal. Let cool before adding.
3. Add the NaOH solution to your bark slurry slowly, stirring
4. Test pH — target is 12-14. Add more NaOH if needed.
5. Solution will turn dark brown/black and may produce mild fumes. Work outdoors.
6. The mixture will be very hot — let it cool before adding solvent.

**Step 3 — First nonpolar pull:**
1. Once cooled to room temperature, add 100ml naphtha to the jar
2. Gently agitate: swirl, do NOT shake vigorously (creates emulsions). Rock slowly for 3-5 minutes.
3. Let layers separate. Naphtha (containing DMT) floats on top. Aqueous layer (dark) sinks.
4. With a turkey baster or pipette, carefully draw off the top naphtha layer into a clean glass container
5. Try not to pull any of the aqueous layer

**Step 4 — Repeat pulls:**
1. Add another 100ml naphtha to the aqueous layer
2. Gently agitate again for 3-5 minutes
3. Let separate. Draw off naphtha layer again, add to first pull
4. Repeat a third time with remaining 100ml naphtha
5. Three pulls extract the majority of available DMT

**Step 5 — Freeze precipitation (preferred):**
1. Place collected naphtha pulls (now called "naphtha soup") into a glass jar with lid
2. Put in freezer overnight (8-12 hours) or longer
3. DMT crashes out of solution as white/yellow crystals
4. Carefully pour off liquid naphtha while still cold — DMT crystals remain stuck to glass
5. Let crystals dry at room temperature in a well-ventilated area

**Alternative — Evaporation method:**
- Pour naphtha soup into glass dish
- Allow to evaporate outdoors or under strong ventilation — NEVER near open flame (naphtha is flammable)
- As naphtha evaporates, DMT crystals form in the dish
- Scrape with razor blade when fully dry

## AAE (Acid-Base Extraction) Method

*More complex but produces cleaner product. Better for bark that hasn't been pre-powdered.*

### Step 1 — Acid step
1. Add bark to water + enough vinegar (or HCl) to reach pH 4-5
2. Simmer gently 2-3 hours (do not boil — use double boiler or low heat)
3. Strain and press out plant material through cheesecloth — save the acidic tea, discard plant matter
4. Repeat acid pull 1-2 more times on plant material, combine all liquid

### Step 2 — Optional naphtha wash
- Add naphtha to acidic tea, stir gently, let separate — discard naphtha layer
- This removes any nonpolar fats/waxes that would contaminate final product

### Step 3 — Basify and pull
- Add NaOH to acidic tea until pH 12-14
- Proceed with naphtha pulls as in STB Steps 3-4

## Product Appearance

**Raw extract ("yellow spice"):**
- Yellow to orange-yellow crystals or waxy material
- Contains DMT + impurities (fatty acids, other alkaloids, plant waxes)
- Fully active. Harsher to smoke than purified.

**Recrystallized ("white spice"):**
- Purified by dissolving raw extract in minimal warm naphtha, re-freezing
- Results in whiter, cleaner crystals with higher DMT concentration
- Smoother to smoke, better vaporization

**N-oxide DMT:**
- Inert unless reduced to DMT — ignore unless doing advanced chemistry
- DCM pulls this more than naphtha — usually not desired

## Solvent Safety

**All solvents used here are flammable:**
- No open flames anywhere near solvents or during evaporation
- No smoking
- Work outdoors or with powerful ventilation
- Store solvents in original containers, away from heat sources
- Dispose of used naphtha by evaporation outdoors in a dish, not down the drain

**NaOH:**
- Wear gloves and eye protection always
- Neutralize spills with vinegar before cleaning
- Do not let it contact skin — flush with water for 15 minutes if it does

**Inhalation:** Minimize inhalation of naphtha vapors. Use outdoors.

## Vaporization (Smoking)

**DMT freebase has a boiling point of ~160°C. It vaporizes efficiently below the combustion point.**

**Methods:**
- **Pipe (Vapor Genie or similar):** Simple. Pack a small amount of plant matter (cannabis, mugwort, mint) in a pipe as a bed. Put DMT on top. Apply heat indirectly — do not combust. Inhale slowly, hold.
- **Machine / oil burner pipe:** Heat the bulb from below, let DMT melt and vaporize. Effective. Thin glass pipes designed for concentrates work.
- **Sandwich method:** Layer between two layers of cannabis (not recommended — destroys potency at higher temp).
- **E-mesh / vaporizers:** Dedicated DMT vaporizers exist. High efficiency. Consistent temperature.

**Dosage (smoked freebase):**
- 10-15mg: threshold
- 20-30mg: moderate experience, strong visuals
- 40-60mg+: full breakthrough (complete ego dissolution, entity contact)

**Technique for breakthrough:** Three large, slow hits. Hold each for 5-10 seconds. At 40-60mg, effects begin on the second or third hit and are complete within 30-60 seconds. You will be unable to hold the pipe — set it down safely before effects peak. Lie back.

## Storage

**DMT freebase:**
- Store in airtight glass container
- Keep dark, cool, away from moisture
- Silica gel packet in container prevents moisture damage
- Shelf life: 1+ years properly stored; degrades slowly if exposed to light, heat, or moisture
- Discoloration (yellowing) = mild oxidation; still active
- Black/brown color = significant degradation; test before using

## Harm Reduction

- **Never use DMT with MAOIs** unless you are explicitly making ayahuasca with full knowledge of MAOI protocols and dietary restrictions. Even small MAOI doses combined with DMT can intensify effects dramatically.
- **No prior experience:** Do not attempt a breakthrough dose as first experience. Work up.
- **Never stand up** during a breakthrough — complete physical incapacitation is common.
- **Set and setting:** Even though it's short, have a safe, comfortable place to lie down.
- **Trip sitter:** Strongly recommended for breakthroughs with someone unfamiliar.
- **Cardiac:** DMT causes brief but significant increases in heart rate and blood pressure. Caution with unmanaged cardiac conditions.
